DayToy Favicon Options

Four directions for the DayToy icon system. Each one keeps the same warm-to-geeky palette, but shifts the meaning: circuit board, pixel toy, orbital playground, and controller-inspired build lab.

Circuit
Most geek-forward. Feels like a product lab mark with PCB traces and a monogram core.
favicon-circuit.svg
Pixel
More playful and game-like. Reads like a retro toy or creative sandbox built from blocks.
favicon-pixel.svg
Orbit
Most "idea playground" of the set. Suggests exploration, experiments, and moving pieces around a core.
favicon-orbit.svg
Playground
Blends geek and toy directly. Feels like a tiny controller, toolbox, and invention bench in one mark.
favicon-playground.svg
